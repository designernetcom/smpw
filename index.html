ABOUT US

We Are An Expert In The Metal Press Work

Established in the year 1979, SMPW is key solution for manufacturing of Automotive and non-automotive with the turnover of 50 Cr. Our diversified product portfolio consists Sheet metal Stamping parts, Welded assemblies, Laser cuttings, Machining, Tubular Parts, Powder coating, light-medium-heavy fabrication. Our core strength lies in Sheet metal stamping and assembly for medium, light and small commercial and passenger vehicles.
