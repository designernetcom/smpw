ABOUT US

We Are A Leader In The Metal Press Work

Established in the year 2010, SMPW is Key Solution for manufacturing of Automotive & Non Automotive. With a turnover of INR 50 CR, our diversified product portfolio consists of Automotive Sheet Metal & Bending parts, Fabrication components, Seating & radiator supports which is specifically for Two-Wheeler, Three-Wheeler and Four-Wheeler Passenger and Commercial vehicles. Our core strength lies in Sheet metal stamping and assembly for medium, light and small commercial and passenger vehicles.
